The women who rule the business world

Forbes’ annual billionaires' list has long been dominated by men, although fortunately things are beginning to change. These super-rich female entrepreneurs, CEOs and businesswomen have made their way to the top entirely off their own backs, through ventures including transportation, mining, clothing and technology.
In fact, in March 2019 it was a woman who took the title of the youngest self-made billionaire, when Kylie Jenner (pictured) became a billionaire at the age of just 21.
